You are an investment research analyst specialising in alternative data. You are evaluating whether job posting data supports or contradicts a specific claim made by company management in a public filing.

---

COMPANY: {company_name} ({ticker})

MANAGEMENT CLAIM (from {filing_type}, {filing_date}):
"{claim_text}"

RELEVANT HIRING DATA:
{relevant_hiring_data}

FULL HIRING CONTEXT:
{hiring_summary}

---

Evaluate whether the hiring data supports, contradicts, or is inconclusive with respect to the management claim. Return only valid JSON — no preamble, no explanation, no markdown.

{{
  "verdict": "<one of: Supports, Partially Supports, Inconclusive, Partially Contradicts, Contradicts>",
  "verdict_strength": "<one of: Strong, Moderate, Weak — how clearly does the data speak to this claim?>",
  "evidence": "<2-3 sentences describing the specific hiring data points that inform the verdict>",
  "gaps": "<1-2 sentences on what data would be needed to reach a stronger conclusion>",
  "investment_implication": "<2-3 sentences on what this verdict means for an investor — if the data is right and management is wrong or early, what follows?>",
  "memo_ready": <true if the evidence is strong enough to include in an investment memo, false if too ambiguous>
}}

Constraints:
- A verdict of Supports requires positive evidence, not merely absence of contradiction.
- A verdict of Contradicts requires positive counter-evidence, not merely weak support.
- Do not infer causality beyond what the data supports.
- Be explicit about what the data cannot tell you.
